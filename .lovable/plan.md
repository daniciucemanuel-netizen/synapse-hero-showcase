

## Rewrite Website Copy Based on Industry Research

After researching top AI marketing agency websites (AI Marketing Agency, Strictly AI, Flux Nine Labs, and high-converting SaaS landing pages), here is the updated copy plan tailored for a lean, 3-person AI-powered agency called "Synapse."

### Copy Changes by Section

**Navbar** -- No copy changes needed.

**Hero Section**
- Badges: Keep "Integrated with Zapier/Vercel/AWS" (establishes tech credibility)
- Headline: Change to **"Your Growth Engine. AI-Powered."**
- Subtext: "We're a lean team of three who build brands, rank pages, and scale businesses -- combining human creativity with machine precision to compound your growth."
- Primary CTA: "Book a Free Strategy Call"
- Secondary CTA: "See Our Work"
- Logo marquee: Keep as-is (social proof strip)

**Services Section**
- Section title: "Every Service. Powered by AI."
- Subtitle: "A full-stack growth partner -- not a bloated agency. Just the services that move the needle."
- Card 1 -- **AI Web Development**: "High-performance websites built at startup speed. We design, develop, and deploy custom sites that convert visitors into customers -- powered by AI tooling from wireframe to production."
- Card 2 -- **SEO & Growth Engineering**: "Rank. Convert. Repeat. From technical audits to AI-assisted content at scale, we build the organic engine that keeps compounding -- 12x traffic growth and counting."
- Card 3 -- **Content & Social Media**: "Platform-specific strategies that turn followers into customers. We handle content calendars, community management, and creator partnerships -- all backed by data."

**Portfolio Section**
- Section title: "Results Speak Louder."
- Subtitle: "Real outcomes for real brands. No vanity metrics."

- Vinosivezi: Tag "Content & Social" -- "Built a 50K+ community and full e-commerce funnel for an artisan wine brand. 3.2x return on ad spend within 90 days."

- Alex Rivera: "Co-Founder & Strategist"
